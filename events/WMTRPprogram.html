Workshop on Model Theoretic Representations in Phonology

Rationale

This workshop brings together researchers studying phonological representations from a computational perspective and the role they play in describing, understanding, and the learning of, phonological processes.

When and Where

  • Dates: September 22-24, 2022
  • Place: Stony Brook University, IACS seminar room (also virtually by zoom, details TBA)
  • There is no fee to register or attend the workshop, but please register for planning purposes.
  • UPDATE: In-person registration is now closed.
