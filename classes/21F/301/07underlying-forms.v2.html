Where do we find the underlying forms?

  1. We have already seen that the isolation form is not reliably the underlying form. (E.g. Russian, Finnish, Saami, Serbo-Croatian)
  2. Next we will see that that the underlying form is not reliably found in any particular morphological context.
  3. Finally, we will see that the underlying form may not be found in any single surface form.
